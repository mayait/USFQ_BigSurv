UPDATE ON PUBLICATION INFORMATION FOR BIGSURV23

Thanks to the partnership that BigSurv has established with IEEE (Region 9, we are offering additional opportunities for sharing your work with the rest of the community. Conference participants can now choose: presentation only; presentation with consideration for publication in the conference proceedings (through IEEE Xplore); or presentation with consideration for publication in a special issue of IEEE LATAM Transactions.

If anyone has already submitted an abstract and would like to take advantage of one of these new opportunities, you will still have the chance to do so. We will be sending a separate email in a few days with information on how to update your submission.

If you still have not submitted your abstract, you can still do so until the extended the abstract submission deadline of March 20, 2023 at https://www.bigsurv.org/conference/register. Spread the word!

We look forward to seeing you in Quito in October 2023!
