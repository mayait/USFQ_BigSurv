2. Newsletter
 

We are attaching to this email our latest edition of the BigSurv Newsletter! In this edition, we share a few of our favorite memories from BigSurv20 (remember all the fun we had during our virtual conference!). We also share some updates and plans for the next conference. We hope you enjoy!
